Tether USD Bridged 3 (3) is a BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain with a total supply of 27,500,000,000 tokens. This document outlines the project vision, tokenomics, roadmap, gaming architecture, governance model, and technical specifications.
01 Introduction & Vision

Decentralized finance has transformed how value is transferred globally, yet accessibility and real utility remain barriers for mainstream adoption. Tether USD Bridged 3 was created to solve both problems simultaneously: by anchoring token value to the US Dollar while building a rich on-chain ecosystem that gives the token genuine, everyday utility.

Our vision is a self-sustaining digital economy where gaming, staking, lending, and governance all converge around a single, liquid asset on BNB Smart Chain.

Core Pillars
  • Stability Reference: Token value referenced to USD, providing a familiar anchor for new and experienced users alike.
  • Real Utility: Every product in the 3 ecosystem requires 3, creating constant organic demand.
  • Deflationary Supply: 2% of every transaction is permanently burned, reducing circulating supply over time.
  • Community First: 85% of total supply allocated to the community through liquidity, staking rewards, and the gaming fund.
  • Cross-Chain Ready: Architecture supports bridges to Ethereum, Polygon, and beyond — one token, many chains.

Deflationary Mechanism

On every 3 transfer, 2% is automatically and permanently burned by the smart contract. This is hardcoded and cannot be disabled by any party. As adoption grows, the burn rate accelerates, creating increasing scarcity and sustained upward price pressure over time.

04 3 Ecosystem

The 3 ecosystem is a fully interconnected suite of products. Each component generates transaction volume that feeds back into the token economy through burns, staking rewards, and prize pool accumulation.

4.1 · 3 Swap

Decentralized exchange for 3 pairs. All swap fees (0.3%) are collected in 3 — 50% burned, 50% redistributed to liquidity providers.

4.2 · Staking Pools

30-day, 90-day, and 365-day lock pools offering up to 300% APY, funded by the staking allocation and ecosystem fees.

4.3 · Decentralized Casino

Fully on-chain casino — roulette, slots, blackjack, and dice. All randomness uses verifiable on-chain RNG (Chainlink VRF). No manipulation possible by the team or any third party.

4.4 · Weekly Mega Jackpot

Automated lottery executed every Sunday at midnight UTC by smart contract. 70% to winners, 20% burned, 10% to development fund.

4.5 · 3 NFTs

5,000 unique NFTs granting VIP casino access, staking multipliers (up to 1.5x), and governance voting power boosts.

4.6 · Cross-Chain Bridge

Non-custodial bridge to Ethereum and Polygon. Users lock 3 on BSC and receive a wrapped equivalent on the destination chain.

4.7 · DAO Governance

On-chain voting for protocol upgrades, fee changes, and treasury allocation. 1 3 = 1 vote. Minimum 10,000 3 to submit a proposal.

07 Governance (DAO)

3 will transition to a fully community-governed protocol in Phase 3 (Q3 2026). The DAO gives token holders direct control over the protocol's future.

  • Voting Power: Every 3 token = 1 vote. NFT holders receive a 2x voting multiplier.
  • Proposal Rights: Any wallet holding at least 10,000 3 can submit a governance proposal.
  • Quorum: Minimum 5% of circulating supply must participate for a vote to be valid.
  • Passing Threshold: Simple majority (>50%) for standard proposals; 66% for protocol upgrades.
  • Time Lock: All approved proposals have a 48-hour timelock before execution.
  • Scope: Fee structures, new game approvals, treasury allocation, partnerships, and protocol upgrades.

09 Security & Audits
  • Contract Verification: The 3 smart contract is fully verified and publicly readable on BscScan.
  • Liquidity Lock: All initial liquidity is locked via a third-party locker, verifiable on-chain.
